Lets get moVenn

While watching Men in Black 2 a few months back I saw an actor who I recognized not by name, but from the Fox comedy Brooklyn Nine Nine. While I could have just gone to IMDB and seek out the common name of my mystery actor I was inspired to write this small application. With it, one can search up to 6 movies or television shows and have a beautiful venn diagram generated showing similar actors between the search items.
The Movie Database API is used to search for actors by movie.
The jVenn jQuery Plugin is used to generate the venn diagrams.
